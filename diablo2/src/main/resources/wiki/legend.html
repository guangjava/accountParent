Hefaetrus is the Amazonian god of fire and rebirth who lives in the Kingdom of Hefaetrus which is located deep within the great volcano, Mount Arnazeus, on the island of Philios. Although primarily the patron deity of farmers, from time to time he bestows his favours upon the warriors of the Islands, so they may keep safe his congregation. Hefaetrus is also husband to the Amazonian god Zerae


Hefaetrus is the source of power behind the following Diablo II Amazon skills:

  • Fire Arrow
  • Exploding Arrow
  • Immolation Arrow

Zerae is the bride of Hefaetrus, and her dominion is vengeance and storms. If an Amazon warrior has proven herself worthy, the goddess Zerae can empower her with the ability to strike down enemies by hurling javelins charged with power as if she had snatched lighting from the heavens.

The Rites of Vengeance are part of Amazon culture, especially concerning the goddess Zerae. Exactly what the rites entails is unknown.

Zerae is the source of power behind the following Diablo II Amazon skills:

  • Power Strike
  • Lightning Bolt
  • Charged Strike
  • Lighning Strike
  • Lightning Fury
