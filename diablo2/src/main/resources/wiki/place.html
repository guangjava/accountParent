The Kingdom of Hefaetrus is the physical domain of the Amazonian god of fire and rebirth, Hefaetrus.

His kingdom is located deep within the great volcano, Mount Arnazeus, on the island of Philios.

Mount Arnazeus is located on the Philios island. It is a volcano inhabited by Hefaetrus, the Amazonian god of fire and rebirth.

菲力奥斯是斯科沃斯群岛之一。Karcheus 山位于这个岛上，是群岛中唯一一个全年可以找到冰雪的岛屿，是 Karcheus the Watcher 的家乡。Arnazeus 山也位于这个岛上。火山上居住着 Hefaetrus，她是火与生的亚马逊之神。
